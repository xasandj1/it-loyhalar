Frequently Asked Questions

What is a UI UX design agency?

A UI/UX design agency is a company, often called a consulting company, that designs digital products of all types and sizes. They have a team of creative experts who ensure building quality UI/UX design that delivers good user experiences. This important to note that creating user experience is a lifeline for B2B and B2C. The design agency using its expertise and time-tested design approach ensures the website is interactive, helping companies achieve their business goals with improvised user experiences (UX) and visually attractive user interfaces (UI). In the general case, a design agency has a separate team of UI and UX designers or may have both.

What services do UI/UX design agencies offer?

A UI/UX design agency offers a range of services centered around digital product user interfaces and user experiences. It can be;

  • User Interface Design Services
  • User Experience Design Services
  • Heuristic Analysis
  • Design Audit
  • Usability Testing
  • UX Research
  • Digital Branding
  • Design System
  • Interaction Design
  • Digital Prototyping

How long does it take to complete a UI/UX design project?

A standard UI/UX design project may take around 1 month to 4 months. However, 4 to 12 or 16 weeks is the average timeline for design projects, the duration can increase based on the type of design product you have and its complexity level. For clear time estimation, you can connect with TheFinch Design Team with your project scope and they let you know the details.

Why do I need a UI UX design company for my business?

The design creates a good first impression and you need experts to help you create that impression. Expert design agencies have vetted resources (UI UX designers) who know the design trends, understand what users want, and also deliver the best experiences. They create products that users love to interact with and fulfill the action flawlessly. A UI/UX design agency helps you improve user experience, brand image, and conversion rates, and deliver higher customer satisfaction.

What is your design process?

We have defined a design process to ensure the digital projects are one of a kind and our client easily differentiate their projects from their competitors. Our design process includes;

User research: We conduct fresh research for each project and its users.

Competitor analysis: We explore competitors, their products, and what they fail to deliver. We find the gap and work to deliver or solve the pain points of real users.

Design: Once we conclude our research and analysis, we design the project. It can begin with prototyping or wireframing as asked by clients or the requirements of the projects.

Testing: Testing is crucial for every project and we ensure the final design project is error-free.

Iterate: Having designed and tested the design project, we keep in touch with the development team and provide design optimization if required.
